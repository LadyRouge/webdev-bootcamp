Kelsey Barrett

Kelsey Barrett is a Colorado native who is now wondering what she's going to do with her life.

Early Life

Born around 10:30pm on Monday October 3, 1988 to Dale and Christine Farmer. She was their 4th child. She looked practically bald to her parents but the skilled eyes of the nurses could tell she was a beautiful redhead.

Education

At the young age of 4, Kelsey was involved in a playgroup with 3 other friends. She then went on to preschool at Holy Trinity Preschool. This was located at a Luthern church. She attented Mark Hopkins Elementary followed by Powell Middle School and then Heritage High School. She then attended Brigham Young University.

Personal Life

High School

Kelsey never dated anyone seriously because she never liked anyone enough. She met her husband, David Barrett, at some point in their childhood. She remembers dancing with him at a Stake dance probably around the age of 15. During both of their senior years, they worked together at Tamarac Medical putting medical testing kits together. David fell for Kelsey, but she was distracted with another boy who was not even good enough for her. It wasn't until years later that she realized she did like David in high school, which may explain why they stayed up every night IMing until late.

College

Kelsey continued to occasionally date but never more than one date with anyone. She always felt like boys acted weird when they were interested so she was never attracted to that and was too impatient to get to know them or could just tell she wouldn't be attracted to them ever. During her freshman year she talked often with David on facebook chat but was always encouraging him to ask out a girl he liked up in Idaho. David left to serve a mission for The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints near the end of her freshman year. That next fall she visited with some friends in Idaho and was reminiscing about high school and working at Tamarac Medical. She decided she was going to write David a friendly letter to see how his mission was going. Months later she received a letter in response and that is the moment she realized she had liked him while in high school. One more letter to him and then lots facebook chatting when he got home brought them together again, but only ever in the same state for a day. They went on a date! They told each other they liked each other a couple weeks later but Kelsey clearly told David, "Don't wait for me, I'm going on my mission." David dutifully dated other girls but none were as wonderful as Kelsey. After serving for 18 months Kelsey returned home and things were awkward between her and David. They quickly resolved that and slowly began going on dates. David was about to give up after her homecoming talk but luckly Kelsey texted him that night. And the rest is history."

Married Life

David and Kelsey were married on August 17, 2012. They were sealed together for time and all eternity in the Denver, Colorado temple. They are devout Denver Nuggett fans even while living in Springville, Utah.
